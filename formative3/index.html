About Me

Hello! My name is Bea, and I am a dedicated 2nd-year Multimedia Arts Student. Welcome to Bea’s Basket — a curated portfolio where I share my work, inspirations, and creative journey as I explore the world of digital design. I love capturing moments through the lens and turning ideas into visual designs that speak to people. Outside of creative work, I enjoy journaling, crocheting, listening to music, and exploring new places for inspiration.
