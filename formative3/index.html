About Me

Hello! My name is Bea, and I am a dedicated 2nd-year Multimedia Arts Student. Welcome to Bea’s Basket! A curated portfolio where I share my work, inspirations, and creative journey as I explore the world of digital design.

I love capturing moments through the lens and turning ideas into visual designs that speak to people. Whether I’m editing a photo, designing a logo, or working on layouts, I enjoy bringing out beauty and meaning in every project.

Outside of my creative work, I enjoy journaling, crocheting, listening to music, watching films, and exploring new places for inspiration.
