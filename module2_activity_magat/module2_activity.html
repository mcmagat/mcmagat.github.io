Flood Control Efforts

Flooding is a recurring problem in the Philippines, especially in Metro Manila and low-lying provinces. The government has been implementing flood control projects such as river dredging, pumping stations, and flood barriers. These efforts aim to protect communities, reduce damage to property, and ensure public safety during typhoons and heavy rainfall.

The Discayas

The "Discayas" issue refers to a recent controversy that sparked nationwide discussion on governance and accountability. It became a trending topic across social media platforms and news outlets, highlighting concerns about leadership, transparency, and the role of citizens in demanding answers from those in power.

The Trillion Peso March

The Trillion Peso March was a massive public demonstration against corruption and misuse of government funds. Citizens from different sectors united to demand accountability and transparency in the use of taxpayers’ money. This protest became a symbol of the Filipino people's call for justice and systemic reform.

Corruption in the Philippines

Corruption continues to be one of the most pressing issues in the Philippines. It affects government services, economic growth, and public trust. Various advocacy groups and citizens are calling for stronger anti-corruption measures, better implementation of laws, and a culture of integrity among public officials.
